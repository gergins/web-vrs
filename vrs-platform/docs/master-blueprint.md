# Master Blueprint

## A. Service Scope
Defines user groups, communication modes, and operational boundaries for VRS delivery.

## B. Architecture
Primary layers:
- client applications
- realtime communication layer
- service orchestration and business logic
- data and storage
- observability and operations

## C. Core Systems
- signaling platform
- SIP gateway
- assignment engine
- session/context management
- workforce forecasting and management

## D. Automation and Skills
Skill-driven control surface for:
- architecture integrity
- callflow and failure-path validation
- accessibility and compliance checks
- AI governance and policy enforcement

## E. Infrastructure
- multi-region deployment strategy
- Kubernetes orchestration model
- Redis + Postgres data services
- global traffic routing and failover

## F. Governance
- strategic steering and funding oversight
- technical review and standards board
- ethics and accessibility governance
- operations governance and incident oversight

## G. Monitoring and Reporting
- SLA dashboards
- QoS and reliability metrics
- compliance and audit reporting

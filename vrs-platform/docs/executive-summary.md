# Executive Summary

## Purpose
Establish a modern Video Relay Service platform enabling Deaf users to communicate through real-time video interpretation with high reliability, accessibility, and regulatory compliance.

## Vision
Deliver a national-grade communication infrastructure that is:
- accessible by design
- highly resilient
- transparent and auditable
- scalable across regions

## Core Capabilities
- real-time video relay (WebRTC + SIP)
- intelligent interpreter assignment
- workforce forecasting
- AI-assisted interpretation support
- multi-region reliability

## Expected Outcomes
- reduced communication barriers
- improved interpreter utilization
- high service uptime (target >99.95%)
- compliance with accessibility and privacy requirements

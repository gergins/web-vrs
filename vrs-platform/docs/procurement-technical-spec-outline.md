# Procurement-Ready Technical Specification Outline

## 1. Service Overview
- VRS service purpose and mandate.
- Target user groups and usage contexts.
- Supported communication modes and channels.

## 2. Functional Requirements
Core capabilities:
- Real-time video relay.
- SIP and WebRTC interoperability.
- Interpreter assignment.
- Call queue management.

## 3. Non-Functional Requirements
Reliability:
- Availability target: >= 99.95%.

Performance:
- Signaling latency target: <150 ms (regional baseline).

Accessibility:
- WCAG-aligned interface requirements.
- Sign-language video clarity threshold requirements.

Security:
- Encryption in transit and at rest.
- Identity and access management controls.

## 4. AI and Automation Requirements
- Transparent decision logic.
- Human override for assistive automation.
- Auditability of AI-assisted outputs.

## 5. Data and Privacy Requirements
- GDPR-aligned processing and retention.
- Consent management requirements.
- Data residency and sovereignty expectations.

## 6. Infrastructure Requirements
- Multi-region deployment capability.
- High-availability signaling architecture.
- Full observability stack requirements.

## 7. Reporting and Transparency Requirements
- SLA reporting package.
- Accessibility KPI reporting.
- Incident disclosure and corrective-action reporting.

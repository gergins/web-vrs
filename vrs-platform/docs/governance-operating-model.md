# Governance and Operating Model

## Governance Layers
### Strategic Governance
Body: VRS Steering Committee

Responsibilities:
- Define service scope and mission.
- Approve budgets and long-term investment.
- Ensure regulatory and public-service alignment.

### Technical Governance
Body: Architecture Review Board

Responsibilities:
- Approve major architecture changes.
- Maintain interoperability and scalability standards.
- Review performance and reliability decisions.

### Ethical and Accessibility Governance
Body: Accessibility and Ethics Council

Responsibilities:
- Review AI usage and human-override behavior.
- Validate Deaf-first UX outcomes.
- Assess equity and inclusion impacts.

### Operational Governance
Body: Service Operations Council

Responsibilities:
- Review incidents and corrective actions.
- Track SLA adherence.
- Oversee capacity and resilience planning.

## Key Operational Roles
| Role | Mission |
|---|---|
| Platform Owner | Overall accountability and governance continuity |
| Product Lead | User value and service evolution |
| Engineering Lead | Delivery quality and architecture execution |
| Reliability Lead | Uptime, resilience, and recovery |
| Compliance Lead | Regulatory and policy alignment |
| Accessibility Lead | Deaf UX integrity and accessibility outcomes |
| Data and AI Lead | Responsible automation and decision quality |

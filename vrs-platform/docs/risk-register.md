# VRS Risk Register

## Technical Risks
| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Signaling outage | Service unavailable | Multi-region failover and HA signaling cluster |
| Media degradation | Poor communication quality | QoS monitoring and adaptive media controls |
| Scaling limits | Queue growth and delays | Horizontal autoscaling and load simulation |

## AI and Decision Risks
| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Assignment bias | Inequity and user harm | Fairness policy checks and periodic audits |
| Incorrect recommendations | Reduced trust and quality | Human override and explainability controls |

## Compliance Risks
| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Data breach | Legal and trust impact | Encryption, IAM, and incident response controls |
| Policy drift | Non-compliance exposure | Scheduled policy/regulatory review cadence |

## Operational Risks
| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Workforce shortage | Service delays and wait times | Forecasting, alerting, and overflow strategy |
| Incident mismanagement | Extended downtime | Incident framework, roles, and runbook drills |

## Strategic Risks
| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Funding instability | Service continuity risk | Multi-year planning and staged investment model |
| Technology obsolescence | Rising cost and fragility | Modular architecture and continuous modernization |
